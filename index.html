Visit Novigrad

Enjoy sorcerers and murderers in this vibrant district close to Oxenfurt.

Top three activities to do at Novigrad

Visit a tavern

Have you ever tried Novigrad beer? It tastes like piss, everyone should try one at least once in their lives!

Attend a Gwent tournament

Gwent is very popular in this city, you can play a game at almost every tavern, why not try your luck in a tournament?

Exchange coin at Vivaldi's bank

One of the finest banks in the known world, the Vivaldi Bank, which has branches in most major cities in the Northern Kingdoms.
